Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our website is to reach more customers who are ready to commit to a life changing experience. We will use content including images, text, trip options, and safety information to engage with potential clients and build revenue.

Audience

Who is our target audience? Our target audience is mainly adults in the age range of 18-60 for our adventures. With our various trip packages we will be able to accommodate people in a large range of financial incomes. Potentially we would also like to expand into corporate retreats and youth trips. What will they find on our website? Our audience will find trip breakdown information, meal information, boating and safety information, and booking information. How will the audience mostl likely engage with our site? We predict our audience will mostly use mobile devices or laptops to access our site.
